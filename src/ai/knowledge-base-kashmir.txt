**Topic: Benefits of Kashmiri Kehwa**

Kehwa is a traditional Kashmiri green tea infused with saffron, cinnamon, cardamom, and almonds. It's not just a warm beverage but a powerhouse of health benefits. Saffron is an excellent source of Vitamin B12 and helps improve immunity. The antioxidants in Kehwa help to moisturize the skin and can reduce the occurrence of acne. It is also known to be a great digestive aid and can help in burning fat, making it an excellent addition to any weight management plan in the cold climate of Kashmir.

**Topic: The Role of Haakh (Collard Greens) in Kashmiri Diet**

Haakh, or collard greens, is a staple vegetable in Kashmir and is prepared in almost every household. It is rich in Vitamin K, A, and C, and is a great source of dietary fiber and iron. Regular consumption of Haak helps in detoxification, supports cardiovascular health by lowering cholesterol, and strengthens bones. For individuals with PCOD/PCOS, its high fiber content helps in managing insulin resistance. It's a low-calorie, nutrient-dense food perfect for a healthy diet.

**Topic: Utilizing Local Nuts and Dry Fruits**

Kashmir is famous for its high-quality walnuts and almonds. These nuts are rich in omega-3 fatty acids, proteins, and healthy fats. Incorporating a handful of these into the daily diet can improve brain function, reduce inflammation, and support heart health. They are an excellent snack for those looking to manage weight as they provide a feeling of fullness.
